About CrowdMonitor

CrowdMonitor is a comprehensive system designed for real-time crowd monitoring, alert generation, and social trend analysis to ensure safety and efficient crowd management.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To provide authorities with real-time insights into crowd density, enabling timely action and preventing accidents or stampedes.

Vision: To create smarter, safer public spaces by combining video analysis, social trends, and predictive modeling.
